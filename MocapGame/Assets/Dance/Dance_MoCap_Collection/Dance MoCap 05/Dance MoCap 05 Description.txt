Dance Package 05

This package features a collection of 10 fbx animations for a bipedal humanoid character performing various dance moves.

Slow Rhythm Dance 11: A series of dance moves to a slow tempo starting low to the ground featuring a variety of leg movements.

Slow Rhythm Dance 12: A series of dance moves to a slow tempo with expressive arm and leg movements.

Slow Rhythm Dance 13: A series of dance moves to a slow tempo featuring dance moves where the character is facing different directions.

Slow Rhythm Dance 14: A series of dance moves to a slow tempo featuring exaggerated upper body movements.

Slow Rhythm Dance 15: A series of dance moves to a slow tempo with sleek arm and hip movements.

Mid Rhythm Dance 10: A series of dance moves to a medium Tempo with exaggerated arm movements covering a moderate amount of space.

Mid Rhythm Dance 11: A series of dance moves to a medium tempo with a variety of arm and leg movements.

Mid Rhythm Dance 12: A series of dance moves to a medium tempo with quick arm movements and multiple side to side steps. 

High Rhythm Dance 13: A series of dance moves to a high tempo featuring a variety of levels including a jump.

High Rhythm Dance 14: A series of dance moves to a high tempo featuring exaggerated movements through the whole body, taking place over a large area, at a variety of levels.
